# Dashboard

The dashboard is your control center for managing your mysrvr.org listing. Access it at [mysrvr.org/dashboard](https://mysrvr.org/dashboard).

---

## Logging In

Click **Login with Discord** in the top right corner. You'll be redirected to Discord's OAuth2 flow and brought back automatically.

<Info>
  mysrvr.org only requests your basic Discord profile and server list. We never ask for your password or sensitive permissions.
</Info>

---

## My Servers

The dashboard shows all servers where you have **Manage Server** permissions:

- **Registered** — Your server has an active listing
- **Not Registered** — Use `/register` in that server to create one

---

## Managing a Listing

Click **Manage** on any registered server to open its settings. From here you can update:

### Subdomain
Change your server's subdomain. Must be available and follow the [subdomain rules](/getting-started#subdomain-rules).

### Description
A short description shown on your listing page.

### Invite URL
The Discord invite shown to visitors. Must be a valid `discord.gg` or `discord.com/invite` link.

### Visibility
Toggle whether your server appears on the public explore page. Your subdomain page remains accessible directly even when hidden.

### Banner URL
An optional banner image shown at the top of your listing page. Must be a direct image URL.

---

## Deleting a Listing

At the bottom of the settings page, click **Delete Listing** to remove your server from mysrvr.org and release your subdomain.

<Warning>
  Deleting your listing is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Warning>
