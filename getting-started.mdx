# Getting Started

Getting your server listed on mysrvr.org takes less than a minute.

---

## Step 1 — Invite the Bot

Invite the mysrvr.org bot to your Discord server. You'll need **Manage Server** permissions to do this.

<Info>
  Only members with Manage Server permissions can register or manage a listing.
</Info>

---

## Step 2 — Register Your Server

Once the bot is in your server, run:

```
/register subdomain:yourserver
```

Replace `yourserver` with your desired subdomain. This becomes your server's address — for example, `yourserver.mysrvr.org`.

### Subdomain Rules

- Must be **3–63 characters** long
- Only **lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens** allowed
- Cannot start or end with a hyphen
- Cannot be a reserved word (e.g. `www`, `api`, `dashboard`)

---

## Step 3 — Visit Your Page

Once registered, your server is live at:

```
https://yoursubdomain.mysrvr.org
```

Manage your listing anytime from the [dashboard](https://mysrvr.org/dashboard).
