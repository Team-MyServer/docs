# Welcome to mysrvr.org

mysrvr.org is a platform for discovering and listing Discord servers — giving every community its own subdomain and page on the web.

---

## What is mysrvr.org?

mysrvr.org lets Discord server owners register their community and get a dedicated page at `yourserver.mysrvr.org`. Visitors can browse listed servers, join communities, and server owners can manage their listing through the dashboard.
